About Our Mission

We are a non profit organization with a commitment to adopt environmentally sound practices to help this wonderful planet we call Earth. We hope to help make our communities foster a regenerative and climate resilient future by recycling e-waste which is a hazardous by product of our technologically advanced lifestyle. The ever obsolete technology has become a necessity in modern times and the constant upgrading of the electronics is the fastest and great contributor to the garbage stream which impact the planet due to its horrendous effects on the environment and living creatures. Our organization advocates the proper disposal of these unwanted electronics by giving a channel to the people in our communities to safely get rid of the their electronic waste. Our mission is to clean up the hazardous pollutants and waste and protect our environment
