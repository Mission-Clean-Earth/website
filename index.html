Mission Clean Earth

Mission Clean Earth has been committed to reducing the chemical pollution on mother Earth by all possible means. We are dedicated to serving our planet by improving environment quality that fosters a sustainable future by partnering with neighboring communities, schools, and industries to work together to create a clean and safe place to live and work for future generations.

Our Initiatives

Our First Initiative in Edison, NJ

We have organized an E-Waste recycling drive in our community. As part of this initiative, we collect E-waste from the doorstep and divert it to the E-Waste recycling centers.
