E-Waste Recycling

We have organized an E-Waste recycling drive creating a bridge between the members of our community and E-Waste recycling centers. Through our initiative, we collect the E-Waste from people who reach out to us and dispose of them through proper channels of recycling. If E-Waste is not properly disposed breaks down and releases toxic metals such as lead, arsenic, etc into the soil and water-polluting the whole environment around it.

Household Hazardous Recycle

To avoid the potential risks associated with household hazardous wastes, it is important that people always monitor the use, storage, and disposal of products with potentially hazardous substances in their homes. Improper disposal of HHW can harm our earth and future generations.
